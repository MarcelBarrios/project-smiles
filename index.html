Non profit organization giving free dental services for everyone at Mineapolis

At Project Smiles, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality dental care, regardless of their age, background, or financial situation. As a nonprofit dental clinic, we are dedicated to providing affordable, comprehensive dental services to individuals and families in our community. Our compassionate team treats each patient with respect and care, ensuring that every visit is personalized to meet their unique needs. Whether you’re here for a routine cleaning or more complex dental work, we’re committed to making your experience as comfortable and stress-free as possible. At Project Smiles, your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we're here to help you smile with confidence!

Our Philosophy

At Project Smiles, we believe in creating lasting relationships with our patients and their families. As a nonprofit dental clinic, we are dedicated to serving everyone, and we work closely with individuals to address their unique dental needs. Our mission is to provide compassionate, accessible care while promoting lifelong oral health. We’re here to not only ensure healthy smiles but also to answer any questions or concerns you may have. At Project Smiles, we’re committed to making quality dental care a reality for all, helping everyone achieve a brighter, healthier future!
