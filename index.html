Non profit organization giving free dental services for everyone at Minneapolis

At Project Smiles, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality dental care, regardless of their age, background, or financial situation. As a nonprofit dental clinic, we are dedicated to providing affordable, comprehensive dental services to individuals and families in our community. Our compassionate team treats each patient with respect and care, ensuring that every visit is personalized to meet their unique needs. Whether you’re here for a routine cleaning or more complex dental work, we’re committed to making your experience as comfortable and stress-free as possible. At Project Smiles, your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we're here to help you smile with confidence!

Our Philosophy

At Project Smiles, we believe in creating lasting relationships with our patients and their families. As a nonprofit dental clinic, we are dedicated to serving everyone, and we work closely with individuals to address their unique dental needs. Our mission is to provide compassionate, accessible care while promoting lifelong oral health. We’re here to not only ensure healthy smiles but also to answer any questions or concerns you may have. At Project Smiles, we’re committed to making quality dental care a reality for all, helping everyone achieve a brighter, healthier future!

Meet the doctors who love caring for you!

Dr. Lando Hall

Growing up, Dr. Hall often visited the dentist for various treatments, from routine cleanings to complex procedures. These positive experiences inspired her to pursue a career in dentistry, with the goal of making a difference in people's lives. Dr. Emily earned her DDS from the University of Minnesota School of Dentistry and later specialized in providing care for underserved communities. Dr. Hall has always connected easily with people of all ages, especially children. Her warm, approachable nature and genuine passion for helping others make every patient feel at ease, creating a welcoming atmosphere for everyone at Project Smiles.

Dr. Barbara Didra

As a child, Dr. Barbara Didra had regular dental visits, which sparked her interest in oral health and the impact it has on overall well-being. Driven by her early experiences, she pursued her DDS from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine, dedicating her career to providing accessible dental care to those in need. Dr. Didra has a special ability to connect with patients of all backgrounds, making them feel comfortable and understood. Her calm, compassionate demeanor is a cornerstone of Project Smiles, where she works tirelessly to ensure that everyone receives the highest quality of care, regardless of their circumstances.
